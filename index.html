More on Life We have to believe what we hear, what we read, what the Word of God says.

Mark 14:21 The Son of man indeed goeth, as it is written of him:

John 6:45 It is written in the prophets, And they shall be all taught of God.

Mark 7:6 He answered and said unto them, Well hath Esaias prophesied of you hypocrites, as it is written,

Luke 7:27 This is he, of whom it is written, Behold, I send my messenger before thy face,

Matthew 4:4 But he answered and said, It is written, Man shall not live by bread alone, but by every word that proceedeth out of the mouth of God.

Matthew 4:7 Jesus said unto him, It is written again, Thou shalt not tempt the Lord thy God. 

Matthew 4:10 Then saith Jesus unto him, Get thee hence, Satan: for it is written, Thou shalt worship the Lord thy God, and him only shalt thou serve.
